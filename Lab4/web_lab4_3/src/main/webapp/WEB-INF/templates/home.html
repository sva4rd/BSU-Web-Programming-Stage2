Car Rental App

Welcome to the car rental app! A wide range of cars are available, which you can rent and return at any time. To rent, leave a request for a free car, after it's processing by the administrator you will be invoiced, after payment of which you will get access to the car. You can view the list of cars you have rented and return it by its ID. Thank you for choosing our application.
